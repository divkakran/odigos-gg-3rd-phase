Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ's for Tourists

Q. Why should I book a private tour guide?

Booking a private tour guide offers you customized and unique traveling experiences. With the help of an expert local guide travellers can gain insider access to their destination with customized, unique tours that address their interests and provide a richer travel experience. The possibilities of experiences with expert local guides are limited only by your own imagination.

Q. Can I contact the tour guide directly?

For privacy, safety and to ensure both parties are protected, provide you with utmost customer service and adhere to our Terms & Conditions, we request all communications prior to booking to be made via our on-site message system.

Q. Must I register to book a private tour guide?

Yes, and it is free! In order to book a tour guide, you will be prompt to either register or sign into your account.

Q. Why must I register to e-mail and/or book a private tour guide?

To ensure guides receive serious inquiries from real people, we require users to register.

Q. How do I pay for my booking?

You have two options to secure your booking:
  • You pay 50% advance online and rest in cash to the tour guide on tour completion
  • You pay 100% payment online

Q. Is the deposit refundable?

Yes, the advance deposit is refundable (Subject to cancellation Policy)

Q. Why should I submit my payment through your system?

Our online payment system is more secure than handling the transaction on your own. We invest a lot into ensuring both parties are protected from fraud and illicit behaviors.

FAQ's for Guides

Q. Why should I register as a private tour guide with Odigos?

Odigos offers an advanced booking service for qualified, local guides. Guides can leverage our sophisticated website and mobile app to list their tours, license, photos, availability and payment details., SMS booking notification, 100% online booking functions and more - making it easy for guides to generate bookings from travelers around the globe. Best of all there are no set-up or maintenance fees for guides.

Q. How much does it cost to register as a tour guide?

Registration of your guiding services is free of charge.

Q. Can I register my guiding services to more than one city?

You can enter in your secondary destination after login to your profile page.

Q. What should I write in my description?

Your description on your profile page should include details of your guiding style, specialty and qualifications. Your description will help clients have a better understanding of who you are, and what your tours will be like.

Q. How will I know if I have a booking request?

You will get notification via SMS and the request will be visible in the app also.

Q. How will clients contact me?

To ensure both parties are protected and adhere to our Terms & Conditions, we request all communications prior to booking to be made via our onsite message system.
Clients will contact you through our onsite messaging system. When there is a new message in your onsite inbox, we will send you a notification.

Q. Can I choose my own guiding rate?

Yes. It is up to the individual guide to decide on their guiding rate that is most fitting to their services
